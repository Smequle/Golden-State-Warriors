The Golden State Warriors

Overview

The Golden State Warriors were founded in 1946 when the NBA was created. They have won seven NBA championships, including in 1947, 2015, 2017, 2018, and 2022. Some of their most popular players throughout history are Stephen Curry, Wilt Chamberlain, Kevin Durant, Klay Thompson, and Chris Mullin. The Warriors also have had the greatest season of all time (73-9). In 2015, the Warriors started a dynasty which led them to five straight NBA Finals. 5 straight is the second highest run in NBA history. Along with those achievements, the Warriors have many Hall of Famers, both current and future.

Big Achievements

The Golden State Warriors have reached many milestones in their 77 years in the league. Their top achievement is probably their number of championships. At seven NBA championships, the Golden State Warriors have the third most NBA championships as a team in NBA History, falling behind the Celtics and the Lakers. They also have the most wins in a season, at 73, when they went 73-9 in the 2015-16 NBA season.
